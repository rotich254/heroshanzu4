SCHOOL STAFF AND DIRECTORS



SCHOOL DIRECTORS

Our school directors are visionary leaders dedicated to fostering a nurturing and innovative learning environment. With a strong commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, they work tirelessly to ensure that our students receive the best education possible. Their leadership and passion for education are evident in every aspect of our school, guiding us towards a bright future.

Our directors are not only visionary leaders in education but also deeply spiritual individuals who believe in the importance of moral and ethical values. They are committed to nurturing both the minds and spirits of our students, ensuring that our school fosters an environment where character development is just as important as academic achievement. Their spiritual guidance helps create a compassionate and supportive community, encouraging students to grow into well-rounded individuals with strong moral foundations.

SCHOOL STAFF

The school has 28 staff who are dedicated professionals who are passionate about teaching and learning. They are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth.

Our staff are not only experts in their fields but also caring individuals who take a genuine interest in the well-being and success of our students.
